Our Story

Founded in the heart of Singapore’s CBD, The Artisan Oven is born from a passion for authentic handcrafted baking. We combine traditional fermentation methods with the finest, locally sourced ingredients to offer a truly premium bakery experience. Our team of skilled bakers work tirelessly each day to craft sourdough breads, delicate pastries, and seasonal cakes that delight your palate, fit for your busy lifestyle.
